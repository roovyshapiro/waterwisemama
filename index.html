Meet Shevy,
Your Water Wise Mama

Hi! I'm Shevy, a Jewish woman, Red Cross Water Safety Instructor, and mama who grew up with the pool as a second home. I've spent my life sharing the calm, joy, and healing that water can bring.

I teach all ages, from babies taking their first splashes to kids building independence and adults learning for fitness, confidence, or pure pleasure. My approach is always comfort first.

I specialize in helping children who carry fear from past experiences, as well as adults who have avoided the water for years. Real safety comes from feeling confident and in control.

I also support Jewish women and mothers who feel anxious about the mikvah. Gently working through water fears can turn a source of dread into an experience of calm and presence.
