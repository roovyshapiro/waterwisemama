Meet Shevy,
Your Water Wise Mama

Hi! I'm Shevy, a Jewish woman, Red Cross Water Safety Instructor, and mama who grew up with the pool as a second home. I've spent my life sharing the calm, joy, and healing that water can bring.

I teach all ages, from babies taking their first splashes to kids building independence and adults learning for fitness, confidence, or pure pleasure. My approach is always comfort first. I meet each swimmer where they are, build trust, and make the water feel safe, warm, and fun again.

I specialize in helping children who carry fear from past experiences, as well as adults who have avoided the water for years. Real safety comes from feeling confident and in control, and that is the kind of safety we build together.

I also support Jewish women and mothers who feel anxious about the mikvah. Gently working through water fears can turn a source of dread into an experience of calm, presence, and even joy, which then becomes a powerful gift to pass on to your children.

If you or your child are ready to break the cycle of fear and create a new story in the water, I would be honored to guide you there.
